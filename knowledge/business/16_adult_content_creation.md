# FanVue/OnlyFans Content Creation Research Findings

## Platform Overview

### FanVue
- **AI-Friendly Platform:** Explicitly allows fully AI-generated content
- **Built-in AI Tools:** Has its own AI image generator (up to 25 images/day)
- **Training Requirements:** 4-20+ photos to train a personal avatar
- **Disclosure Required:** Must clearly disclose AI-generated content
- **Face-swap Allowed:** Can use deepfake/face-swap but must verify real model

### OnlyFans
- **More Conservative:** Stricter content policies
- **No Built-in AI:** Creators must use external tools
- **Higher Competition:** 3-4 million+ creators

## What Content Sells Best

### Top Revenue-Generating Content Types

1. **Lingerie & Boudoir** (High Revenue)
   - Most popular and profitable category
   - Lower barrier than explicit content
   - Wide audience appeal

2. **Fitness Content** (Semi-nude/Nude) (High Revenue)
   - Large market for workout content
   - Can be combined with other niches
   - Authentic, aspirational appeal

3. **Feet Content** (High Revenue, Low Effort)
   - Thousands per month for top creators
   - No face required (anonymity)
   - Image-based (easier than video)

4. **Cosplay & Roleplay** (Medium-High Revenue)
   - Huge niche on OnlyFans
   - Character-based content (anime, games, etc.)
   - Holiday themes (Halloween, Christmas)

5. **Couple Content** (High Revenue)
   - Massive market for couple content
   - Top creators often feature couples

6. **Custom Content** (Premium Pricing)
   - Most lucrative per-item
   - Fans provide the ideas
   - Often the best-performing content

7. **Behind-the-Scenes** (Engagement Booster)
   - Long-form video content
   - Builds personal connection
   - Keeps subscribers engaged

## Content Strategy for Maximum Revenue

### The 80/20 Rule
- **80% Free/Subscription Content:** Keep subscribers engaged
- **20% PPV (Pay-Per-View):** Save the spiciest, most extreme content for DMs

### Content Mix Recommendations

| Content Type | Frequency | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lingerie/Boudoir Photos** | Daily | Core content, keeps feed active |
| **Behind-the-Scenes** | 2-3x/week | Builds connection, long-form video |
| **Fitness Content** | 2-3x/week | Taps into fitness niche |
| **Cosplay/Themed** | 1x/week | Variety, seasonal appeal |
| **Custom Content** | On-demand | Premium pricing, high revenue |
| **Q&A/Live Streams** | 1x/week | Engagement, retention |
| **PPV Content (DMs)** | 2-3x/week | Most explicit, highest revenue per item |

### Variety is Key
- **Switch backgrounds frequently:** Bedroom, bathroom, living room, kitchen
- **Change outfits:** Different lingerie sets, colors, styles
- **Vary poses:** Standing, sitting, reclining, over-shoulder
- **Different times of day:** Morning light, golden hour, nighttime

## Technical Best Practices

### Image Quality Requirements
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1080p, ideally 4K
- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred, ring lights for nighttime
- **Equipment:** Modern smartphone sufficient to start, upgrade to DSLR later

### AI Generation Best Practices
- **Training Data:** 20+ high-quality photos showing different angles and expressions
- **Consistency:** Use the same base model/avatar for character consistency
- **Variety in Prompts:** Change poses, outfits, settings to avoid repetitive content

### Content Safety
- **Disclosure:** Always disclose AI-generated content (FanVue requirement)
- **Avoid Explicit Faces:** Use your own verified face for face-swap (FanVue policy)
- **Stay Within TOS:** Both platforms prohibit illegal content

## Monetization Strategies

### Pricing Tiers
- **Subscription:** $9.99-$29.99/month (most common: $14.99-$19.99)
- **PPV Content:** $5-$50 per item (depending on explicitness)
- **Custom Content:** $50-$500+ (depending on complexity and exclusivity)

### Revenue Boosters
1. **PPV in DMs:** Send exclusive content directly to subscribers
2. **Tip Menus:** Offer specific content for tips
3. **Bundles:** Package multiple items at a discount
4. **Limited-Time Offers:** Create urgency
5. **Polls:** Let fans vote on next content (increases engagement)

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Lingerie/boudoir content is the sweet spot** - high revenue, wide appeal, lower barrier than explicit content
2. **Variety prevents subscriber churn** - mix content types, settings, and themes
3. **PPV content in DMs is where the real money is** - save the best for premium pricing
4. **AI tools make it scalable** - FanVue's built-in generator allows 25 images/day
5. **Consistency + Quality = Success** - post regularly, maintain high image quality
6. **Engagement = Retention** - Q&As, live streams, and custom content build loyalty
