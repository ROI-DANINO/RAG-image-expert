**Your First Week on FanVue - Content Schedule, Pricing, and Strategy**

## Part 1: Your First Week - Content Schedule

Your goal in Week 1 is to establish a posting rhythm, build an initial subscriber base, and start generating revenue through Pay-Per-View (PPV) content.

### Core Strategy

**Post Frequency:** 1-2 times per day on your main feed. Use Stories for casual updates, polls, and engagement.

**AI Generation Strategy:** FanVue allows you to generate up to 25 AI images per day. Use this quota strategically by creating content 2-3 days in advance. This builds a backlog and ensures you always have high-quality content ready to post.

**Content Mix:** Follow the 80/20 rule. Approximately 80% of your content should be accessible to all subscribers (feed posts), while 20% should be premium PPV content sent via Direct Messages.

## Part 2: Pricing & Monetization Menu

Your pricing should be competitive for a new creator while still being profitable. The goal is to attract subscribers with a reasonable monthly fee, then upsell them with high-value PPV and custom content.

### Subscription Pricing

| Tier | Price | What Subscribers Get |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Monthly Subscription** | **$14.99/month** | Access to all feed posts (1-2 per day), ability to send DMs, participation in polls and Q&As. |
| **3-Month Bundle** | **$38.22 (15% off)** | Same as monthly, but rewards longer-term commitment. |
| **6-Month Bundle** | **$67.45 (25% off)** | Best value for fans who want to support you long-term. |

**Launch Promotion:** Offer a **50% discount for the first month** to new subscribers during your first week. This is a powerful tool to convert your initial followers into paying fans. Promote this heavily on social media.

### Pay-Per-View (PPV) Menu

## Part 3: Launch Strategy & Tips

### Week 1 Goals

1.  **Gain 20-50 subscribers.** Use your social media to promote your FanVue page. Offer the 50% discount for the first month.
2.  **Send 2 PPV messages.** Track your conversion rate (how many subscribers unlock the PPV content). Aim for a 10-20% conversion rate initially.
3.  **Establish your posting rhythm.** Consistency is key. Stick to your schedule.
4.  **Engage with your fans.** Respond to DMs, answer questions in Stories, and make your subscribers feel valued.

### Promotion Tips

-   **Use Instagram, Twitter/X, and TikTok** to tease your FanVue content. Post SFW (safe-for-work) versions of your images with a link to your FanVue in your bio.
-   **Use hashtags:** #FanVue, #AICreator, #ContentCreator, etc.
-   **Engage with other creators:** Comment on their posts, collaborate if possible.

### Content Tips

-   **Variety is key.** Don't post the same pose or outfit every day. Use your 25 daily AI generations to experiment.
-   **Listen to your fans.** Use polls to ask what they want to see. Create content based on their feedback.
-   **Quality over quantity.** It's better to post 1 high-quality image than 5 mediocre ones.

### Monetization Tips

-   **PPV is your main revenue source.** Don't be afraid to send PPV messages 2-3 times per week.
-   **Price strategically.** Start with lower prices to build trust, then gradually increase as you gain more fans.
-   **Upsell.** If a fan buys a $10 PPV set, send them a message a few days later offering a $20 custom set.
