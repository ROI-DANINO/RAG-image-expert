# Boudoir Pose Research for AI Prompting

## Key Findings: What Makes Effective Boudoir Poses

### Core Principles
1. **Curves are essential** - Adding curves to the female body is flattering and aesthetically pleasing
2. **Back shots are safe** - Not imposing, doesn't demand too much, face not visible
3. **Hand placement matters** - Fingers convey tension or relaxation
4. **S-curves work** - Positioning body parts in opposite directions creates flattering S-curve
5. **Arching the back** - Creates sensuality and emphasizes curves

## Top Boudoir Poses for AI Prompting

### 1. Arms Up / Hair Fixing
**Description:** Model lifts both arms as if fixing hair
**Works well with:** Loose top, sweater (reveals stomach/hips when arms lift)
**Prompt keywords:** `arms raised above head`, `fixing hair`, `hands in hair`, `cheeky reveal of stomach`

### 2. Lying on Back - S-Curve
**Description:** Lying on back, head turned to one side, legs slightly to opposite direction
**Creates:** S-curve in body
**Prompt keywords:** `lying on back`, `head turned to side`, `S-curve body position`, `legs positioned opposite to face`

### 3. Back Arch / Crawling Pose
**Description:** On hands and knees, arched back, kicked foot
**Sensuality:** Arch of back, movement like a cat prowling
**Prompt keywords:** `on all fours`, `arched back`, `cat-like pose`, `one foot kicked up`

### 4. Side-Lying with Splayed Hair
**Description:** Lying on side, head on arm, hair splayed out
**Prompt keywords:** `lying on side`, `head resting on arm`, `hair splayed out around head`

### 5. Back Shot - Curve Emphasis
**Description:** Shot from behind, emphasizing back curve
**Why it works:** Subtle, artistic, safe for beginners, highlights curves
**Prompt keywords:** `shot from behind`, `gentle curve of back`, `over-the-shoulder look`, `back view`

### 6. Simple Kneeling
**Description:** Kneeling on bed, looking slightly away
**Why it works:** Not exaggerated, helps ease into shoot
**Prompt keywords:** `kneeling on bed`, `looking away from camera`, `relaxed hand position`

### 7. Sitting on Edge (Furniture)
**Description:** Using arm of sofa/couch as support, creating curve along hips and back
**Prompt keywords:** `sitting on edge of furniture`, `leaning on arm of couch`, `curve along hips and back`

## What to Avoid in Prompts

1. **Stiff, unnatural poses** - Always include "natural", "relaxed", "comfortable"
2. **Tense hands** - Specify relaxed hand positioning
3. **Flat angles** - Always emphasize curves and body positioning
4. **Direct frontal only** - Mix angles (side, back, over-shoulder)
5. **Static poses** - Add movement keywords like "fixing hair", "adjusting strap"
